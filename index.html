Georgetown, Kentucky development changes between the years of 2011 and 2021

Project details

The area chosen for this project is Georgetown, Kentucky located in central Kentucky. This project visualizes rapid development in the region during 2011-2021.

The goal of this project is to visually examine rapid changes in 4 area of intrests(aoi). This data can help predict future development trends for the region.

Visualizations created from lidar and raster data provided by KyFromAbove in ArcGIS Pro. Cesium Ion was used for the slideshow with data created by Harrison Siegel.

Page and visualizations created by Harrison Siegel for GEO 409, Department of Geography, University of Kentucky. May 2025.
